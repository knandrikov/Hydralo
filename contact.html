What is project "HYDRALO":

What is project “Hydralo”? There are many people living on Earth but someday there just wont be enough space for all of us. Sea is barely untouched. In fact we have explored 5% of the World ocean and that’s such a small exploration. So project “Hydralo” is about making smart homes, bases, cities, research labs and etc. underwater. This will be a huge change for many humans, because most of their time will be underwater. Many reasons gave our inspiration in making this project. With it we will:
    • More personal space and less overpopulation • More ecological planet • Better life • Better research on underwater life beings and the ocean
